About Us

The healthcare sector has undergone significant transformations in recent years. However, despite these advancements, accessing pharmacy products remains a cumbersome process for many individuals. Long queues, limited inventory, overpriced pharmaceuticals, and restricted operating hours pose significant barriers in obtaining essential medication and healthcare supplies. MedConnect seeks to dismantle these barriers by "connecting" the power of digital innovation to with the pharmacy experience overall speeding it up, the main problem we want to fix is the price of pharmaceutical drugs; this can make it extremely difficult for the lower and middle class to purchase medicine they may need. A second problem was apparent from the pandemic; when people are sick, they might be unable to physically go into stores. Another thing would be not putting other people at risk, if I'm sick I'm conscious of my coughs, sneezes, and who I'm around, MedConnect completely erases that need and streamlines the process.
